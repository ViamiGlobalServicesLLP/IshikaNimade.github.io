Company Profile

COMMITED TO COMMITMENT

We are a Logistics solutions & service provider offering end-to-end logistics services globally which enables our business partners to manage their business worldwide. Our strategic freight services via Air & Ocean for Import & Export, are aptly complemented by Custom Clearances & last mile delivery to customers factory/warehouse. Warehousing is another activity that is offered by us for our esteemed customers based on specific requirements.

A vibrant relationship with global logistics partners  enables us to satisfy  diverse needs under a Single Window concept. Our Focus & initiation  has always been towards cultivation of  vertical specific core competencies within our organisation structure to cater to the Global requirements and need of times.

We cherish relationships & have evolved in execution with excellence to ensure our clients are provide with

“Maximum Value - faster & better - always!!!”
